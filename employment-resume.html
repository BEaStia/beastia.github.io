How I make engineering scale

I build clarity, ownership, and feedback loops into the way teams work—not as process for its own sake, but as leverage for delivery.

  1. Clarify directionMake priorities, constraints, decisions, and delivery risks visible.
  2. Build strong teamsHire deliberately, onboard well, develop engineers, and grow leaders.
  3. Design ownershipCreate boundaries where teams can own outcomes without constant escalation.
  4. Deliver consistentlyUse planning, review, incident learning, and quality gates that match the product risk.
